About Loligang

Originally a Discord server created for the Youtube channel Squashyboy, we are now a community of anime, custom keyboard and osu! enthusiasts with a sizable amount of friendly active members! We offer a variety of chat channels for different interests, from Minecraft to V-Tubers to technology to food!
